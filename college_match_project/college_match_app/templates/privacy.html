Privacy Policy

At College Match, we value and respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use any of our services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Personal details like your name and email when you reach out to us.
  • Academic data like your marks or stream to provide predictions.
  • Browser data like IP address, device type, and usage patterns through cookies and analytics.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data helps us to:

  • Provide college prediction and guidance.
  • Connect you with consultancy services if needed.
  • Recommend scholarships and resources relevant to your interests.
  • Improve our website and services.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We may share it only:

  • With consultants you choose to connect with.
  • With service providers for email and hosting (under strict privacy terms).
  • If legally required by law enforcement or government agencies.

4. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies to enhance user experience and track anonymous analytics. You can manage cookie settings in your browser at any time.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal data. However, no method is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute protection.

6. Third-Party Links

Our website may link to external websites (e.g., scholarship portals or consultancy partners). We are not responsible for their content or privacy policies.

7. Your Choices

You can request access to or deletion of your data by contacting us. You can also unsubscribe from any promotional communication at any time.

8. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please reach out through our About Us page or email support.
