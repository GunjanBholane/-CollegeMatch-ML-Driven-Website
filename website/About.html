About Us

Who We Are

We are a group of students currently enrolled in college, pursuing various degrees, and some of us are graduates. Our goal is to help students make informed decisions about their education and career. This website aims to assist students in finding suitable engineering colleges, understanding eligibility, and accessing essential resources for their journey.

How to Use the Website

Use our website to:

  • Check your eligibility for top colleges based on your marks and caste.
  • Stay updated with the latest news about entrance exams, rules, and regulations.
  • Explore scholarships in the "Scholarships" section.
  • Find out if you are directly eligible for top colleges in the "Dream College" section.
